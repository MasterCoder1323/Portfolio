Places to Go

Okemo Mountain Resort

Feb. 5 2023 - Feb. 9 2023

At Okemo Mountain resort I stayed in a trailside room. There are lots of fun trails for all ages. You can rent ski boots and skis for the season. I used a season pass. Okemo is part of vail and it is packed on the weekends and neerly empty during the summer. If you want to be in a fancy hotell I recomend Jacksongore wich is part of okemo mountain. If you want the best skiing you should go to Okemo clocktower base for loging.


Kenedy Space Center

2017

Kenedy Space Center Is where they launch rockets in florida. You can see the rockets launch, and even eat lunch with a astronaut. You can walk under a huge rocket sudpended by ropes and poles.

The Movie is worth watching. I have watched it many times. It never fails to make me laugh. The minions do some very very crazy things. You must watch it. The movie lasts about 1 hour and 30 minutes.

Tron: the original

Very Intense. Will keep you at the edge of your seat

Tron is a good movie to watch. It was the best movie of its time. Its about a computer program taking over the computer world an a group of programs stop him. Once you start you wont want to stop watching it, so start early because the movie is 1 hour and 36 minutes.
